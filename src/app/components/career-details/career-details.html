UX Designer

Job Details
  • Job Title: UI/UX Designer
  • Location: Pune, Maharashtra (On-site)
  • Job Type: Full-time, Permanent
  • Salary: ₹5,00,000 – ₹7,00,000 per year
  • Schedule: Day Shift, Monday to Friday
Benefits
  • ✔️ Health Insurance
  • ✔️ Provident Fund
  • ✔️ Flexible Schedule
Job Overview

We are seeking a talented and creative UI/UX Designer with 5+ years of experience to join our team. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to design intuitive and user-friendly interfaces for web and mobile applications. The ideal candidate is passionate about design, deeply understands user behavior, and has a track record of crafting seamless digital experiences.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to define user requirements and business goals.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing.
  • Design wireframes, prototypes, user flows, and high-fidelity mockups.
  • Create and maintain design systems for consistency.
  • Iterate on designs based on feedback and testing results.
  • Stay updated with design trends and best practices.
  • Present and defend design decisions to stakeholders.
  • Work with developers to ensure accurate implementation.
  • Identify opportunities to enhance user experience.
  • Conduct research & analysis for project requirements.
  • Create structured project flow presentations.
  • Focus on detailed planning to minimize rework.
  • Develop domain-specific presentation decks.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript (a plus).
  • Experience in designing for accessibility.
  • Familiarity with design systems and component libraries.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Design, HCI, or related field (or equivalent experience).

Why Join Us?

  • Work with a dynamic, innovative, and collaborative team.
  • Competitive salary and benefits.
  • Flexible working hours.
  • Growth and career advancement opportunities.
